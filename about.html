                     It's also low in added sugars, saturated fat, and salt. 
                    Foods to include
                    Fruits and vegetables: Eat lots of fruits and vegetables, especially dark green leafy vegetables 
                    Whole grains: Choose whole grains like whole-wheat bread, brown rice, and whole-grain pasta 
                    Lean proteins: Eat fish, poultry, tofu, beans, lentils, eggs, and Greek yogurt 
                    Healthy fats: Choose healthy fats like olive oil, chia seeds, groundnuts, and seeds
